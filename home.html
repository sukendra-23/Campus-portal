About College Events

Our College Event Management Portal is designed to bring together students, faculty, and the entire campus community. We organize a diverse range of events throughout the academic year, including technical festivals, cultural nights, sports championships, career fairs, and much more.

Our mission is to create memorable experiences, foster learning, and build a vibrant campus community. Whether you're interested in technology, arts, sports, or career development, we have something exciting for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse our events page, select the event you're interested in, and click the "Register Now" button. You'll be prompted to provide your details, and the registration will be confirmed instantly.

Is there a registration fee for events?

Most events are free for students. Some special events or workshops may have a nominal fee, which will be clearly mentioned in the event details. All fees are collected on-site during the event.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration through your dashboard. However, please note that cancellations should be made at least 24 hours before the event date to allow other students to register.

How will I receive event updates?

Once you register for an event, you'll receive updates via email. You can also check your dashboard for the latest information about your registered events.

What if I miss an event I registered for?

If you're unable to attend an event, please cancel your registration so others can participate. Repeated no-shows may affect your ability to register for future events.
